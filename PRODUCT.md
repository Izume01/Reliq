# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Individuals and developers looking to share sensitive information securely without leaving a permanent digital footprint.

## Product Purpose
A secure platform for sharing sensitive messages and files that expire automatically. It focuses on one-time viewing, self-destructing links, and absolute privacy.

## Brand Personality
Stealthy, safe, secure, dark-themed, and trustworthy.

## Anti-references
Bright/light-themed applications, cluttered layouts, noisy dashboards, and generic bright SaaS templates.

## Design Principles
- Stealth by default: The UI should feel discrete and unobtrusive.
- Expert confidence: The application should communicate security and reliability through its visual restraint.
- Focus on the secret: Remove any unnecessary distractions from the core action of creating or viewing a secret.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain high contrast ratios despite the dark theme. Ensure forms are accessible and errors are clearly communicated without relying solely on color.
